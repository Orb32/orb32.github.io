A mini display that's compact with features and fully customizable.

Orb32 A1 mini is compact with features for people who want a small always on dashboard. Its fully customizable, so you can decide what shows up and how it looks perfect for a desk, workshop, or bedside setup. With WiFi built in, it can display stock, crypto, and forex graphs, live weather, all in a clean, easy to read interface. The rotary knob and Back button make navigation fast and tactile, so you can move through views without fuss.

• Custom your own firmware • Updates firmware every month • 7 hours battery life
1. Esp32 c3 super mini chip

160 MHz 32-bit RISC-V single-core processor. 2.4GHz wifi Bluetooth 5.0 and 4 MB memory with 400KB SRAM 384 KB ROM.

2. 2.4 tft screen

2.4 inch with 4 - SPI RGB screen ST7789 driver.

3. EC11 knob

EC11 Rotary Encoder 360 degree continuous rotation with its own push button.

4. Push button

Push and released momentary switch use to turn on Orb32 A1 mini.

5. 1x 18650 lithium battery

3.7V 9000mAh rechargeable (Li-ion) cell long battery life with low power Esp32 c3 super mini chip.

6. TP4056 battery charger

Linear charging IC/module 5.5 V USB type C fast charge not supported.

7. Power switch

Use to cut off power directly from battery to the chip.
